Systematic Trade Journalization

OptionsBook is much more than just a simple notebook for your trades. Take your options and stock trading a huge step further by systematically logging and tracking all your transactions. OptionsBook allows you to

enter and update all your orders with limit or stop prices

execute orders that provide accurate execution data such as prices for both one-legged and multi-legged option strategies.

monitor break-even prices for each individual position.

immediately display the position risk both in USD and as a percentage of your net liquidity without time-consuming manual calculation steps.

Individual Position Tracking


With OptionsBook you can track each position completely independently.

OptionsBook automatically recognizes the strategy types and displays them, depending on the composition of the legs involved within each position. 

OptionsBook makes it easy to distinguish between several independent positions within one and the same underlying. This distinguishes OptionsBook from the most common brokerage platforms

Each credit and debit and each dividend is tracked in relation to its respective position and is also used to calculate the break-even prices of the positions.
